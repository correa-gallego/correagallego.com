About

I am an undergraduate Biology student at Universidad EAFIT interested in understanding microbial life in extreme environments. My research focuses on microbial ecology, geomicrobiology, and the biogeochemical processes that sustain life under harsh physicochemical conditions.

Previously, I worked as a Visiting Student Intern at the ECSO Lab, Purdue University, where I gained hands-on experience in environmental and computational microbiology.

Research Interests

Microbial Ecology Extreme Environments Geomicrobiology Biogeochemistry Environmental Microbiology
